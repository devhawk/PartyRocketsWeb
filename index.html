About the Party Rockets

The Party Rockets is a team of six creative elementary school kids from Redmond, Washington. They compete in Destination Imagination, a global competition of imagination and innovation. The Party Rockets won first place at the 2015 Seattle-Metro regional tournament and tied for first place at the Washington state tournament. They will be competing at the Destination Imagination 2015 Global Finals.

Destination Imagination exists to inspire and equip students to become the next generation of innovators and leaders. Every year, over 200,000 students - from kindergarteners up to university seniors - around the world form teams and take on one of seven challenges. These teams spend the better part of the school year working on their solutions - prototyping ideas, building props and backdrops, writing scripts and honing their improv skills.

This year, the Party Rockets chose to compete in the Science challenge. The challenge was called Making Waves and involved the following:

  • Building an incredible sound machine that produced two different sounds.
  • Presenting an eight minute story about the sound machine that included a change to a faster or slower narrative pace.
  • Integrating two visible displays of sound waves into the presentation.
  • Including two additional choice elements that show off the teamâ€™s interests, skills, areas of strength, and talents.

Destination Imagination teams get to show off their solution at qualifier tournaments that are held in early spring. The best teams at these tournaments are invited to compete at Global Finals - the worldâ€™s largest celebration of creativity. Each May, 8,000 plus students from around the world decend on the University of Knoxville to show off their solutions and get a chance to mingle with their peers from around the globe. The Party Rockets are honored to be invited to compete at Global Finals and look forward to the opportuntity to show the world what they can do!

The Party Rockets Need Your Help!

While the opportunity to compete at Destination Imagination Global Finals is a huge honor, it is also fairly expensive. Beyond the obvious costs of flights to and hotel rooms in Tennessee, there is also significant cost for entering the tournament itself and shipping the team's props to Knoxville. We estimate it's going to cost upwards of $12,000 to enter the tournament, get the team (plus their coach, team manager and props) to Knoxville, put them up in a hotel and feed them while they're there.

If you'd like to help sponsor the Party Rocket's trip to Global Finals this year, you can make a tax-deductible donation to the Washington Imagination Network (aka W.I.N.) on behalf of the Party Rockets. W.I.N. is the Washington state affiliate organization for Destination Imagination and is a 501(c3) corporation. 100% of the money donated to W.I.N. in the team's name - as well as any employer matching funds - will go to cover the team expenditures for Global Finals. Any money over and above those costs (should we be so lucky!) will stay with W.I.N. to assist them in their mission to provide creative problem-solving opportunities for students in Washington state.

Credit card donations can be made online at the W.I.N. Click And Pledge page. Check donations can be sent to W.I.N. at P.O. Box 1738, Coupeville, WA 98239-1738. The Washington Imagination Network Tax Id is 91-1728778. Please include our team name "Party Rockets" and team number "147-80976" on any donations you make to ensure it makes its way to us!

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to email us @ PartyRockets@outlook.com or contact the team managers Julianne Pierson and Tracy Crowley.
