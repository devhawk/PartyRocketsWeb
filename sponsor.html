Sponsorship

Ready to Perform

The Party Rockets are looking for donations to help defray the costs of competing in the Destination Imagination tournament

If you'd like to help sponsor the Party Rockets trip this year, you can make a tax-deductible donation to the Washington Imagination Network (aka W.I.N.) in the Awesome Assemblers name. W.I.N. is the Washington state affiliate organization for Destination ImagiNation. 100% of the money donated to W.I.N. in the team's name - as well as any employer matching funds - will go to cover the team expenditures. Any money over and above the competion costs (should we be so lucky!) will stay with W.I.N. to assist them in their mission to provide creative problem-solving opportunities for the youth of Washington state.

To help sponsor the Awesome Assemblers trip to Global Finals, please mail a check to:
